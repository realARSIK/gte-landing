Standing tall against the desert backdrop, these colossal structures are a photographer's paradise. If you're looking for natural wonders, the Red Sea Riviera is the place to be. Its crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs provide endless opportunities for underwater photography. From colorful marine life to shipwrecks, these underwater landscapes are sure to impress.
